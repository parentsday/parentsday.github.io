About

The Parents Day

The Parents Day is the day that the PC works for all year. The Parents Day Committee (PC) organises the annual Parents Day. On this day, the parents of AI students can get to know our study programme, the university, and CognAC. This way they get a peak in the daily lives of their kid(s).

Opening

HG 0.307

Enjoy a presentation of the study association CognAC and the Parents Day Committee. They will go through the schedule with you and explain what the rest of the day will look like!
