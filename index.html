The Family Day

Every year, the Family Day Committee Committee (FamCo) organises a day where all AI students are invited to show their family what our study is about. This way, family members can get first-hand experience into what studying AI is like, and what CognAC as a study association has to offer. It will be a day filled with various lectures given by staff, and various relaxed activities organised by CognAC.

Opening

HG 00.307

To start of the day in a low-key manner, study association CognAC and the Family Day Committee will introduce themselves. After this introduction, you will be familiarised with the schedule of the day, have gained some insight into the university and most importantly: what CognAC has to offer in terms of activities and other services!

Introductory AI Lecture

Dr. Frank Léoné

HG 00.307

To experience what it is like to study AI, you will first have to understand what AI actually is. As it it far more than just ChatGPT and funny robots. AI is a field that is constantly evolving and growing field. It is developing so quickly, that it is hard to keep track of. Dr. Frank Léoné will introduce you to this fasciniating topic.
