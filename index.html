Currently, the Family Day Committee is still busy finalising the day, so expect some changes to be made to the website in the next few weeks. Specifically, some activities on the day may be moved around in the schedule a bit.

The Family Day

Every year, the Family Day Committee Committee (FamCo) organises a day where all AI students are invited to show their family what our study is about. This way, family members can get first-hand experience into what studying AI is like, and what CognAC as a study association has to offer. It will be a day filled with various lectures given by staff, and various relaxed activities organised by CognAC.
